What is SCAS?

Sumulong College of Arts and Sciences is founded by Victor R. Sumulong with the help of his own family that envision to establish an educational institutions that will help Antipolenos to cope with the emerging education of the Philippines.

SCAS offers a diverse selections of programs spanning various fields, equipping students with a with a well-rounded education and the skills needed to excel in today's dynamic world.

Sumulong College Of Arts and Sciences foster good and quality education for all the dreams to be shape and thrive. With the help of the people, Sumulong College of Arts and Sciences establish in the late 2008 were education is thriving the most.
